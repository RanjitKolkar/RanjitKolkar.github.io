About

I am a Assistant Professor and Head of Artificial Intelligence at Srinivas University Institute of Engineering and Technology. I am interested in the Internet of Things and Artificial Itelligence. I have carried out an research in Human Activity and Pattern recognition using multimodal sensor in smart home environement.
