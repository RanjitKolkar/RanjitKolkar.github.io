About

Dr. Ranjit P. Kolkar is an Assistant Professor at National Forensic Sciences University, Goa Campus. He holds a Ph.D. from NITK Surathkal with research on multimodal sensor smart homes, human activity recognition and behavioural pattern mining. His expertise spans Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, Digital Forensics, IoT systems, data analytics and applied machine learning.

He has academic, industry and research experience across teaching, software development, institutional leadership and national capacity-building programs.

Ongoing Research Projects & Open Innovation Initiatives

Current research focuses on applying Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, Digital Forensics, Computer Vision, and Intelligent Analytics to solve real-world investigative, security, governance, and automation challenges.

AI-Powered Forensic Video Analysis System

Development of an intelligent forensic video analytics platform designed to reduce investigator time in reviewing CCTV, bodycam, and surveillance footage. The system automatically detects suspicious motion, identifies persons/vehicles, tracks movement, patterns, highlights anomalies, extracts timestamps, and generates summaries.

Technologies: Computer Vision, YOLO, Face Detection, Object Tracking, OCR, AI Summarization

Deepfake Detection & Media Authenticity Framework

Research on identifying manipulated videos, synthetic audio, AI-generated images, and face-swapped media used in cybercrime, misinformation, extortion, and fraud. The framework combines frequency-domain analysis, biometric inconsistencies, voice mismatch detection, and explainable AI.

Focus Areas: GAN Detection, Facial Artifact Analysis, Audio Verification, Trust Scoring

Cryptocurrency Investigation & Risk Intelligence Framework

A structured investigation framework for tracing suspicious cryptocurrency transactions, fraud wallets, scam ecosystems, laundering routes, mixer usage, and exchange movement patterns. The system supports law enforcement and regulatory agencies in crypto intelligence operations.

Areas: Blockchain Analytics, Wallet Clustering, Scam Detection, Compliance Intelligence

Blockchain-Based Evidence Management System

Designing a tamper-proof digital evidence chain-of-custody platform using blockchain. Ensures secure storage, timestamping, access control, integrity verification, and transparent evidence transfer across investigators, labs, and courts.

Applications: Digital Forensics, Legal Evidence Handling, Secure Audit Trails

Drone Flight Path Visualization & Threat Mapping

Intelligent visualization platform for analysing drone flight paths, suspicious route patterns, no-fly zone violations, swarm activity, and geo-spatial movement reconstruction. Useful for border security, smart cities, and event surveillance.

Technologies: GIS Mapping, Trajectory Analytics, Geo-Fencing, Risk Prediction

AI-Based Financial Fraud Detection Platform

Ongoing work on identifying fraudulent transactions, shell accounts, abnormal fund flows, mule networks, and suspicious banking behavior using machine learning and graph intelligence.

Focus: Banking Analytics, Tax Investigation, Transaction Intelligence

Smart Sensor Behaviour Analytics for Elderly Care

Extending Ph.D. research toward real-time monitoring of elderly residents using multimodal sensors to detect falls, abnormal inactivity, health-risk behaviour, and routine deviation in smart homes.

Domain: Healthcare AI, IoT Sensors, Ambient Assisted Living
