About

Dr. Ranjit Kolkar is an Assistant Professor at the National Forensic Sciences University (NFSU), Goa Campus. He holds a Ph.D. from NITK Surathkal and specializes in Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, Digital Forensics, Blockchain Forensics, and Telecom-enabled Fraud Investigation.

He is actively engaged in UK–India collaborative initiatives on Telecom Fraud Mitigation and contributes to international cybercrime strategy discussions. His work integrates AI-driven analytics, blockchain investigation frameworks, OSINT intelligence, and forensic evidence-based methodologies.

He has trained Cyber Commandos under the I4C initiative (Government of India), Income Tax officers, banking professionals, Indian Army personnel, and law enforcement agencies in cybercrime investigation and digital forensic practices.

Research Vision: Building AI-driven forensic intelligence labs for national security and cyber resilience.
